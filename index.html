About Us

Afrikode Tech Solutions Limited is a Nigerian-born, Africa-focused technology company committed to bridging the continent's digital divide. We are a team of developers, educators, strategists, and innovators creating context-aware, secure, and scalable tech solutions that power progress across sectors, from education and healthcare to finance and governance.

At Afrikode Tech Solutions Limited, we believe technology should be inclusive, accessible, and transformative. Founded by a passionate industry expert and renowned Lecturer, our mission is to ensure that Nigeria, starting with its northern region, is not left behind in the global tech revolution.

Our debut project is an educational platform that complements traditional university learning. Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our History

Afrikode was founded by a passionate software engineer and lecturer who saw firsthand how students in Northern Nigeria struggled with digital access. What started as a mission to improve education through technology has grown into a leading provider of solutions that address Africa's unique infrastructural and digital challenges.

Our Mission

To build inclusive, impactful digital solutions that solve Africa's real-world problems and empower its people, institutions, and industries.

Our Vision

Our Work Showcase

Educational Platform

Our flagship educational platform complements traditional university learning by providing students with digestible lecture contents including notes, videos, and assessments.

The platform bridges the communication gap between lecturers and large student populations without replacing the classroom experience.

Features include offline access for low-bandwidth regions, interactive assessments, and progress tracking for both students and educators.

Healthcare Management System

Our healthcare solution streamlines patient management, medical records, and appointment scheduling for clinics and hospitals across Nigeria.

The system includes offline capabilities for regions with limited connectivity and integrates with existing healthcare infrastructure.

Key features include secure patient data management, prescription tracking, and telemedicine capabilities for remote consultations.

Financial Inclusion Platform

Our fintech solution enables secure digital transactions and financial services for underserved communities across Africa.

The platform includes wallet integration, payment processing, and transaction management with enterprise-grade security.

Designed with an offline-first approach, it works seamlessly even in areas with limited internet connectivity.

Enterprise Resource Planning

Our custom ERP solution helps African businesses streamline operations, manage resources, and improve efficiency.

The system integrates inventory management, HR functions, accounting, and customer relationship management in one platform.

Built with scalability in mind, it grows with your business and adapts to the unique challenges of African markets.
